Hello!

We are Killa Bites

Killa Bites was born at a kitchen table much like your own, except this one was covered in cake bombs and popcorn. When life threw Laura lemons in the form of an ALS diagnosis for her husband, she needed a plan for her future. She could make lemonade or, in this case, chocolate. After months of recipe tweaking and taste-testing, Laura was ready to launch her sweet business. Armed with support from her family and friends, she embarked on a journey to make her dreams come true. Donna and her scrumptious biscotti soon joined the party, and the ladies have since been busy baking and marketing their irresistible cake bombs, biscotti and popcorn.

Laura is a visionary, engineer, chocoholic, wife, and mother.

Donna is a magician in the kitchen, runner, wife and mother.

Killa Bites is Proud to Support Charitable Organizations in Our Community! We give to a variety of non-profits in the Vancouver, Washington areaincluding the following:

  • Church on the Rock
  • Doernbecher Children's Hospital
  • Ridgefield Lions Charities
